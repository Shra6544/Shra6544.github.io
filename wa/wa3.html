Introduction

Vox Machina is the adventuring party featured in the first campaign of the popular Dungeons & Dragons web series, Critical Role.

The party consists of various colorful characters who embark on numerous adventures in the world of Exandria, guided by Dungeon Master Matthew Mercer.

Originally formed as a mercenary group, they became legendary heroes, defeating numerous powerful foes, including the Chroma Conclave and Vecna.

History of Vox Machina

Vox Machina began as a group of adventurers based in the Tal'Dorei city of Emon.

Their earliest adventures were small in scale, but over time, they encountered significant threats that had global implications.

One of their major threats was the Chroma Conclave, a collective of powerful dragons. The party gained fame through their victories, ultimately being named champions of Tal'Dorei.

Throughout their journey, they built strong bonds, faced personal losses, and evolved from a ragtag group into heroes of legend.

Significance in Critical Role

Vox Machina played a pivotal role in the success of Critical Role, helping to popularize live-streamed D&D campaigns.

Each character brought something unique to the table:

  • Vex'ahlia, the half-elf ranger, and her twin brother, Vax'ildan, the rogue, added emotional depth with their sibling dynamics.
  • Keyleth, the druid, represented an ongoing journey of self-discovery and leadership.
  • Percy, the gunslinger, brought a dark, revenge-driven storyline that resonated with fans.
  • Grog, the goliath barbarian, added humor and heart.
  • Scanlan, the bard, was known for his quick wit and charisma.
  • Pike, the cleric, was a source of healing and moral support for the team.

Together, they became a beloved part of the D&D community, setting the tone for future Critical Role campaigns.

Notable Campaigns and Adventures

Among Vox Machina's most famous adventures are:

  • The Briarwood Arc: A personal quest for Percy, involving revenge on the rulers who had murdered his family.
  • The Chroma Conclave Arc: The group faced a terrifying collective of chromatic dragons intent on domination.
  • The Rise of Vecna Arc: The final and most dangerous foe, where the group had to stop the ascension of the lich-god Vecna.

Legacy and Future Impact

Vox Machina's story continues to influence fans and creators alike.

They were central in the animated adaptation The Legend of Vox Machina, which premiered on Amazon Prime Video.

The characters remain beloved in the Critical Role universe, with their legacy occasionally referenced in the show’s subsequent campaigns.

They’ve left a lasting impact on the broader D&D community, inspiring countless fan creations, from fan art to entire homebrewed campaigns.
